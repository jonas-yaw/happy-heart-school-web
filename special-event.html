Excursion

Every term, as much as practicable, we try to arrange one form of excursion or the other away from the usual place of study for the children. We are mindful in selecting age-appropriate locations and also distances that do not wear out the kids unduly.
Apart from for the kids having fun in the company of their peers outside their normal school premises, we aim at making these trips very exciting while providing an important source of variety, discovery and learning for them even at their young ages.

Benefits

  • Getting in touch with real world
  • Adventure and exploration
  • Erasure of classroom boredom
    • Holiday

    This is optional and targeted to support parents that have engagements during the holiday period. Holiday school creates room for the development of the child’s learning, thinking and creativity skills. It keeps the child a step ahead of the world.
    At our holiday school we carry out activities that help the children gain extra-curricular aptitudes to complement their normal classroom instruction.

    Benefits

  • Learning
  • Bonding and friendship
