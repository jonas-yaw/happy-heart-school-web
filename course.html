Classes

CRÈCHE

Children should have started walking before acceptance into the crèche. Unfortunately we are unable at the present time to take in babies that are yet to walk.

  • Physical Health Development
  • Emotional and Social Development
  • Cognitive and Language Development
  • Music, Art and Creativity.

    • NURSERY

    The nursery group consists of students typically of average ages two to three years. The subject areas include:

  • Emotional and Social Development
  • Cognitive and Language Development
  • Music, Art, and Creativity
  • Letters and Numbers
  • Nature and Environment

    • KINDERGARTEN

    There are two years of Kindergarten, namely KG1 and KG2, typically for those in age bracket 4 to 5 years respectively. Normally, students in KG2 would be ready to begin Primary 1 as they would be approaching the age of 6.

  • Reading and Numbers
  • General Science
  • Sensorial
  • Personal Development

    • PRIMARY

    The primary school curriculum is a build-up upon the preschool activities, and at this point ICT, foreign language(s) and local Ghanaian languages are introduced as additions to Maths and Science.

  • Mathematics and Science
  • ICT and Creative Art
  • French and Ghanaian Language

    • AFTER-SCHOOL CARE

    We recognize that parents would occasionally be faced with heavy work schedules or unforeseen circumstances that make it impossible for them to pick up their ward(s) on time. Parents are free to make arrangements with the School Administration for extended caretaking of the children for a late pick-up charge.
