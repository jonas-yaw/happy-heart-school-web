The Directors

Isaac Asante

CEO

Isaac is Chief Executive Officer of HHS providing strategic leadership in the management of the School. He currently provides direction in financial management and strategy, and is responsible for developing frameworks and policies which support programing around children.
For many years he worked in the field of international development, managing large multi-year grants from major government donors including USAID, CIDA, DFID as well as private corporate grants like the conrad N Hilton foundation. He has worked with major international non-governmental organisations (INGOs) with a ministry focus on children, notably World Vision and Plan International, and has also worked with FHI 360, Chemonics, and Social Impact.
Mr. Asante holds an MSc in Strategic Management from the University of Derby, UK. Apart from extensive work life in his home country Ghana, he has lived and worked for many years in a numebr of East and West African countries and the Middle East.

Harriet Asante

Headmistress

Harriet is the Director of School Operations. As Head of School, she is responsible for administration and human resource development. She is virtually the face of HHS, interacting with the staff, teachers, parents and the general public as far as the day to day operations of the school is concerned. She oversees the welfare, professional aptitude and performance of Teachers, and ensures that teaching and learning happen in the desired direction.
Harriet has worked as Information Manager with The British Council in Ghana. She is an alumnus of the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), and also holds an MBA in Project Management from the Ghana Institute of Management and Public Administration (GIMPA).
She has lived and worked in Ghana and the UK and has sojourned for a few years in Uganda.
